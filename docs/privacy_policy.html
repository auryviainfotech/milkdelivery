Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Milk Delivery ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Phone number, name, email address
  • Delivery Address: Your address including PIN code for delivery services
  • Payment Information: Payment details processed through Razorpay (we do not store card details)

Location Information

We collect precise location data to:

  • Provide accurate delivery services
  • Auto-fill your delivery address
  • Connect you with nearby delivery partners

Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Order history and subscription details

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our delivery services
  • To process your orders and payments
  • To communicate with you about your orders
  • To send you promotional offers (with your consent)

4. Data Sharing

We share your information with:

  • Delivery Partners: Your address and order details for delivery
  • Payment Processors: Razorpay for payment processing
  • Service Providers: Supabase for data storage and authentication

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and stored securely in Supabase's cloud infrastructure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
